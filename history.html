History Nkuzi is a non-profit section 21 company (97/20743/08) providing a range of support services to historically-disadvantaged communities wishing to improve their rights and access to land. Nkuzi started operating early in 1997 and had 18 full time staff working throughout the Limpopo Province, northern parts of Mpumalanga Province and with farm residents in Gauteng Province.Nkuzi has engaged with the threelegs of land reform (Restitution, Redistribution and Tenure Reform) in Limpopo Province since its establishment in 1997.From May 2000 Nkuzi has also worked with farm residents in the Gauteng Province. Nkuzi Development Association is a member of the chapter of the South African NGO Coalition (SANGOCO) in Limpopo Province. Our role in thecoalition is of a reference organization on matters of land and agriculture and also leading that sector within the coalition. Furthermore Nkuzi is providing a secretariat support to the coalitionby housing its officials.
